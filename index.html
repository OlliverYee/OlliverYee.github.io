Why CCS?

CCS has been developed by leading software development company Entuitive. Working closely with large Hazardous Materials Assessment companies has allowed us to gain constant feedback on what they would like to see in features of the software. The initial release targets asbestos survey companies allowing them to complete surveys on tablets or phones in the field, or on a live web portal (for multiple surveyors on one building).
