2016 Health and Safety at Work

Asbestos regulations mean that workplaces must effectively manage the risk of asbestos. Buildings constructed prior to 2000 are likely to contain asbestos materials which must be managed in accordance with these regulations.

The Solution

BMIS uses information in an asbestos register to automatically create a live asbestos management plan for the workplace. The management plans are designed to meet regulatory requirements and the approved code of practice.

BMIS includes risk ratings for asbestos items which have been developed using algorithms. Management actions assigned to asbestos items can be planned into the system and key contacts are reminded when inspections or removals are due.

BMIS acts as a repository for all asbestos information relevant to the building. Air monitoring data, clearance certificates for removed asbestos, workers asbestos certificates and task analysis are all managed in the system.

Contractors can log into BMIS via QR coded stickers on buildings to see a summary of the management plan, which informs where the asbestos items are located and what work can be undertaken. of practice.

Contractor logins are supported by an online training module to ensure that contractors fully understand their legal responsibilities as well as how to read a management plan and identify asbestos.

Management Plans

BMIS produces management plans in accordance with asbestos regulations.

Duty 13 of the regulation states that a management plan must be produced and maintained for each workplace where asbestos is present.

BMIS acts as a repository for all asbestos information relevant to the building. Air monitoring data, clearance certificates for removed asbestos, workers asbestos certificates and task analysis are all managed in the system.

As all plans have to be kept up to date, this may result in a constant re-issue of management plans from a consultant.

BMIS manages it at the push of a button, with updates to asbestos management in the building easy a new management plan can be issued in minutes.

All previous management plans are saved within the system and the new plans are accessible to all workers through asbestos identification stickers on building doors.
